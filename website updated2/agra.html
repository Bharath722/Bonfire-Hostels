Why Agra?

Visit Agra to see a whole phase of Mughal Empire.The 17th century Taj Mahal in Agra is the most popular monument in India, attracting over 2.5 million visitors per year. Agra is home to four World Heritage Sites in Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Sikandra & the Fatehpur Sikri. Agra is also home to many other beautiful Mughal buildings like Akbar's Tomb, Itmad-Ud-Daulah etc. Dayal Bagh is an under-construction temple that many visit. The lifelike carving in marble is not seen anywhere else in India..
