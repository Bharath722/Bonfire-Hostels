About Us

"Launching India's first ever backpacker hostel chain to introduce Bamboo Bunk beds !!!"

Bonfire Hostels is the India's first ever backpacking and travelling oriented backpacker hostel chain. A place to bring out the travelling freak inside you. Known for it's great mix of a relaxed and family atmosphere with a real backpacking vibe, it gives you an Interactive Backpacking Experience with breathtaking views of the city as all the activities and places are around the corners of the hostel. We provide a unique, beautiful and fun place for all travelers as we have transformed the building into a friendly and social playground for travelers.

Bonfire is here to cater all the backpacker's travelling to India by providing the cheapest hostel accommodation in the city with the best luxurious facilities. Well its not just yet we are really close to all the historical places (Only 500 meters away from TAJ MAHAL.. It's a Secrete Sssshhh ) We have depicted the whole Agra on our cool graffiti and a Cool hangout zones to chill and relax. Bonfire provides everything from a cool Rooftop cafe & Restaurant to sit and enjoy your meals to Cool hangout areas ,from garden yoga to Bamboo Sunbath beds to lie down and get those lovely smothering sun rays and from coozy dorms to luxurious privates. Get attached with your soul and refresh and re- energize with the coolest backpacker hostel in Agra..!

Free Wi-fi

Know what's there for you to explore in the city on the internet as we provide free high speed internet 24x7.

Fun Room

Enjoy your stay at the hostel by accessing our in-house play room. Sit on the sofa and enjoy the soccer match or go for a chess match on the big-sized chess table.

Secure Lockers

Enjoy your stay without any worries as we make sure that your personal belongings are safe in our secure lockers.

Luxury Rooms

Not a fan of the bunk bed. We provide luxury private rooms to cater to your need.

Library

Spend your time by going through our awesome inventory of books. Who knows you might find a good read for the rest of your journey.
